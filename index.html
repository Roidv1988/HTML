About Me

Hi, I'm Roi Dvide, 35 years old from Rosh Ha'Ain. Married and father of 1.
I am a homely, family person. I like to host and spend time with friends. I like to watch series, especially action and suspense, and I like to cook and bake cakes.
Today I work as a studio manager - graphic artist and performance artist in the field of packaging and pharma, folders, brochures, flyers, CDs, calendars, etc.

I studied design and branding at Kevim College - Kfar Saba and also courses through the IDF.
Before the army, I studied film and television engineering with a specialization in video editing and after effects.

In the army I was in charge of video in a military printing house. Film photography and editing, training and professional advice for new and veteran soldiers, ongoing maintenance of editing computers and editing software. After that i was charged on command and management of the department numbering between 6-10 soldiers, the guidance and training of soldiers in the field of graphic design and printing, work and service with customers, receiving projects and accompanying the soldiers to work until finished product. promoting and leading structural changes in the department, working with suppliers to purchase new machines.

I'm Highly motivated, open minded to expanding horizons, good and quick perception, Ability to manage and lead a team, with responsibility.
Beyond being a graphic artist, I have knowledge and extensive control in the world of printing and professional printing productions.
Little experience also in the WEB world, landing pages and publications for Facebook. Independent learner software for designing apps and websites (Figma and XD).
A variety of printing machines, development in darkrooms, plate machine, format printing Wide (plotters - sunshades and canvases, rollups and caps), prints on materials (Arizona), TPI management (customer service, work orders, entry supervision) of projects until they are delivered to the customer.
